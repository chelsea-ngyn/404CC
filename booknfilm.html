The Hunger Games

In the nation of Panem, Katniss Everdeen volunteers to take her sister’s place in the deadly Hunger Games, a televised fight to the death. Trapped in the arena, she must rely on her survival skills while navigating treacherous alliances and moral dilemmas. The Hunger Games is a tense, thought-provoking story of survival, sacrifice, and resistance against a brutal regime.
