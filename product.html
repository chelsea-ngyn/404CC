The Hunger Games

In a dystopian future, the nation of Panem is divided into 12 districts, each forced to send a boy and a girl to compete in the annual Hunger Games—a televised fight to the death. Katniss Everdeen, a resourceful teenager from District 12, volunteers in place of her sister to face the brutal arena. As alliances are formed and the stakes grow higher, Katniss must rely on her instincts and survival skills to navigate the deadly game, all while struggling with the moral cost of victory. The Hunger Games is a gripping tale of survival, sacrifice, and the power of defiance against a ruthless system.
